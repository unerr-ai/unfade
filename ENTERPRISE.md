# Unfade Enterprise (coming soon)

Unfade is **open source** today: local capture, distillation, MCP, dashboard, and leadership-style exports run entirely on the developer machine.

**Enterprise** adds org-ready capabilities for teams that need governance, scale, and support—without changing the local-first contract for individual engineers.

## Planned capabilities

| Area | What we’re building |
|------|----------------------|
| **Team dashboards** | Aggregated direction density, comprehension trends, and spend proxies across repos—with explicit opt-in and redaction policies. |
| **Compliance exports** | Audit-oriented bundles: methodology, numeric rollups, evidence chains where policy allows—aligned with export ethics in the OSS product. |
| **SSO / SAML** | Sign-in for org-hosted consoles and optional cloud sync boundaries. |
| **Cloud-hosted global index** | Optional server-side rollup for large polyrepo fleets, with the same metrics definitions as local `global_index`. |
| **Priority support & SLA** | Response-time targets and onboarding for regulated industries. |

## Who it’s for

- Engineering orgs standardizing on AI-assisted development who need **explainable** metrics, not vanity dashboards.
- Companies in **India and other regions** balancing DPDPA-style privacy expectations with leadership visibility into AI spend and reasoning quality.
